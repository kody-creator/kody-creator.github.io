About Tesla

Tesla was first founded in 2003 by a group of engineers who wanted to prove that electric cars can be practical. Tesla's goal is to accelerate the global transition by using sustainable energy. It first launched the Tesla Roadster in 2008, followed by a series of other models, such as the Model S, Model 3, and Model X. Currently, Tesla is one of the world's leading EV companies and continues to innovate in electric vehicle technology, battery technology, and autonomous driving systems.

Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is a mid-sized electric sedan made by Tesla. It was designed to be more affordable than the Model S so that more people can use electric cars. Model 3 Launched in 2017, it is the most-selling Tesla vehicle for its affordable price. It features long range, high stability, and the latest software. The Model 3 has a unique minimal design that allows you to operate the vehicle using a central touchscreen.

Cybertruck

Cybertruck is an electric pickup truck launched by Tesla. Cybertruck was first unveiled in 2019 and has a futuristic design and strong performance. The characteristics of the Cybertruck are that it is made of an external steel structure, so it is very durable and can be used for off-road or work.In addition, Cybertruck is also an electric vehicle, so it has strong torque, fast acceleration, and long driving range.
